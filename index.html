About Us

This a charity that helps the poor and the ones who need warm love and home. We are from Cape Town, started this organisation in 2016 so basically we are sitting on a 5th year this year. We started this organisation after we saw that there was a need of people who will be taking care of the kids who needed a warm love. We decided to open a charity called Help | Uncedo, because we saw that these children needed "uncedo" so they can also feeel loved.



We started by looking out for those who are in need and first year we got 5 children that will always be in the streets always looking worried, they would sleep in the streets even though there's a rain. So we took them and explained to them that we will be now on taking care of them an dalso take them to school. The others don't even have parents and never saw them n their lives. This to me was a sorrow, I asked God to please give me strenght to be able to help all of them.



People would always ask if our organisationis registerd, it's not registerd because we are still trying our luck to get registerd, and we are looking for sponsor's to sponsor our organisation so we can be able to work over the world. We are still based in Cape Town which is not good becausethere are a lot of children out there who need to be loved.

We do hope that we will get help or sponsors so we can reach all the kids who needs a warm love even warm home. We've already took the oters to school so these poor kids can see that there are people out here who are kind. We still want more kids so our charitycan grow big and also for people to donate with us in whatever we are struggling to do.



The Charity will forever appriciate the ones who will put hands in it because we do need more people who will be kind and raise these poor kids in a big house and warm. I feel like there are many kids who needs love outside and kids who needs to be taken care, so I guess this charity has been a life saviour to others and will still be.
